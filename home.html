MISSION


To revolutionize disease detection in agricultureindustry by developing and deploying smart disease detection technology especially for corn crops. We aim to provide farmers with an advances and efficient solution to detect diseases in the corn fields, enabling them to make timely and informed decisions to protect their crops and maximize their yields.

VISSION


To become the leading provider of smart disease detection system for corn crops worldwide. We envision a future where farmers can proactively manage and combat diseases in their corn fields, resulting in healthier plants, increased productivity, and sustain farming practices. By harnessing the power of technology, we strive to contribute to global food security and the well-being of farming communities.

Web Features


Image Classifier

An image classifier is a feature on a website that uses machine learning algorithms to analyze and categorize images based on their content. It can automatically identify and assign corn disease. Users can upload an image to the website, and the image classifier will process it and provid predictions about the posibble disease of the image.



Video Classifier

Similar to the image classifier, a video classifier is a feature that also applies machine learning techniques to analyze and categorize videos based on the plant characteristics. Users can take a live video by allowing the website to access the phone camera and the video classifier will process it, often by analyzing the video, it will provide insights or predictions about the posibble disease of the plant.



Corn Disease Directory

A corn disease directory is a feature on a website that serves as a comprehensive reference or database of various diseases that affect corn plants. It provides information about different types of diseases, their symptoms, causes, and possible treatments. The directory may include images or descriptions of the diseases, as well as information about preventive measures or management strategies. Users, such as farmers or agricultural researchers, can access the directory to identify and learn about specific corn diseases they encounter in their fields, aiding in early detection and appropriate action to mitigate the impact on crop yields.
